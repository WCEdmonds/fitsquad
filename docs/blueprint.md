# **App Name**: UnitFit Planner

## Core Features:

- Data Input: Collect Soldier fitness and health data (AFT scores, run times, health info) through a structured form.
- Tiered Grouping: Dynamically split soldiers into smaller groups based on needs and deficiencies in terms of fitness metrics.
- AI Workout Plan Generation: Use an AI tool to generate tailored workout plans for sections, squads, platoons, or companies based on collected data, specific needs, and goals.
- Workout Plan Display: Present workout plans in a clear, actionable format, highlighting specific exercises, sets, reps, and goals for each group.
- Progress Tracking: Allow Soldiers to track their progress within the generated workout plans, noting completed exercises and improvements in performance.
- Performance Reporting: Allow commanders to track the progress of the soldiers.
- Database: A database to store data

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A muted green (#4B5320) to convey trust and reliability, evoking a sense of calm and focus, aligning with military professionalism.
- Background color: Light grey (#F0F0F0), creating a clean and neutral base for the content.
- Accent color: A vivid orange (#FF8533), contrasting with the primary blue to draw attention to key interactive elements and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif offering a modern look combined with a little warmth.
- Use clear, modern icons to represent different fitness metrics and workout activities, enhancing usability and engagement.
- Design a responsive, grid-based layout to ensure the app is accessible and effective across various devices.
- Implement subtle transitions and feedback animations to acknowledge user interactions and guide them through the workout planning and tracking process.
